About Me 

Greetings! My name is Ty, and I had the privilege of growing up on the beautiful Caribbean Island of Antigua. Raised by a strong single mother, we later moved to the United States of America when I was 11 years old in pursuit of a better life and education. Although there were challenges along the way, things worked out in the end.

From a young age, I developed a deep fascination with technology and computers. Gaming also became a significant part of my life, but my curiosity extended beyond playing games themselves; I was captivated by how they actually worked. This passion ignited my determination to become a game designer in the near future. I experimented with programming languages like Lua and Java to create my own games, but due to my limited experience at the time, they didn't quite meet my intended vision.

As I explored game design further, I discovered a new interest in the artistic aspects of the field. I began to hone my drawing skills, realizing that creating content, whether through games, art, comics, or videos, was my true calling. With the unwavering support of my mother, I decided to take a step towards turning my passion into a reality.

After graduating from high school, I made the decision to enroll at the esteemed College of Westchester, majoring in Digital Media. This educational journey will provide me with the knowledge and skills necessary to bring my creative visions to life.

I am excited for the future and determined to make a meaningful impact in the world of digital media.
